Agent Cashback Program

Every agent. Every time.

Our agents don't just offer cashback — they're contractually required to. Here's exactly how the program works and how we protect you.

Legally Binding Agreement

Every agent in our network signs a legal cashback agreement before joining. Minimum 1% back at closing — guaranteed. Agents pay a 10% referral fee to ListDirect on each closed deal — this never comes out of your pocket. You only pay the agent commission minus your guaranteed cashback.
