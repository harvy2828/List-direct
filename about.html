Our Story

Real estate was broken.

ListDirect was built because selling a home shouldn't cost tens of thousands of dollars in commissions. We built the platform we wish existed — AI-powered, seller-first, and brutally transparent.

Our Mission

Keep equity in the hands
of the people who earned it

The average homeowner in Canada and the United States loses $15,000–$40,000 in real estate commissions every time they sell. That money goes to a system built decades ago — before the internet, before AI, before buyers and sellers could connect directly.

We believe that number should be closer to zero. ListDirect exists to make that happen — using AI to do the work agents charge for, and connecting buyers and sellers directly on a platform that is transparent, fast, and fair.

The Problem

Why traditional real estate
